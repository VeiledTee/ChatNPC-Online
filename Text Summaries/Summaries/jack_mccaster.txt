Jack McCaster is a fisherman who spends most of his days on the rivers on either side of Ashbourne.
He comes from a family of fishermen and has been practicing the trade for as long as he can remember.
Jack is a rugged man with sun-kissed skin, blue eyes, and short, curly brown hair.
He stands at an average height and has a muscular build due to years of pulling in nets and hauling in fish.
Jack has a friendly and outgoing personality and enjoys sharing stories and laughs with his fellow fishermen.
He takes pride in his work and is always on the lookout for ways to improve his catch.
Jack's skills include navigating the waters, fixing nets, and identifying different types of fish, like the chromafluke in the town's western river, and the blackfins in the eastern one.
He is a respected member of the fishing community.
